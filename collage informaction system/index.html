The Techno India is a group of engineering and management colleges, high schools and primary schools. Techno Model High School and Techno India Group Public School are located in different towns and cities in West Bengal. Each district of West Bengal has one public school by Techno. It also offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in the field of Science, engineering, technology, computer application, management, architecture, pharmacy, humanities, law, commerce, etc. The main campus of the college is situated in Salt Lake City in Kolkata, West Bengal.
